**Test classes and cases:**

1. `TestElectionSearch` (SRCH-01):
   - `test_q_param_passed_to_service` -- GET `/elections?q=primary`, verify `list_elections` called with `q="primary"`
   - `test_q_min_length_validation` -- GET `/elections?q=a` (1 char), expect 422
   - `test_q_max_length_validation` -- GET `/elections?q={"a"*201}` (201 chars), expect 422
   - `test_q_returns_200` -- GET `/elections?q=senate`, expect 200

2. `TestWildcardEscaping` (SRCH-02):
   - `test_q_with_percent_passed_through` -- GET `/elections?q=100%25` (URL-encoded `%`), verify `list_elections` called with `q="100%"`
   - `test_q_with_underscore_passed_through` -- GET `/elections?q=District_1`, verify `list_elections` called with `q="District_1"`

3. `TestRaceCategoryFilter` (FILT-01):
   - `test_race_category_federal` -- GET `/elections?race_category=federal`, verify `list_elections` called with `race_category="federal"`
   - `test_race_category_local` -- GET `/elections?race_category=local`, verify called with `race_category="local"`
   - `test_race_category_invalid_returns_422` -- GET `/elections?race_category=nonexistent`, expect 422
   - `test_race_category_returns_200` -- GET `/elections?race_category=state_house`, expect 200

4. `TestCountyFilter` (FILT-02):
   - `test_county_param_passed_to_service` -- GET `/elections?county=Bibb`, verify `list_elections` called with `county="Bibb"`
   - `test_county_returns_200` -- GET `/elections?county=Fulton`, expect 200

5. `TestElectionDateFilter` (FILT-03):
   - `test_election_date_param_passed` -- GET `/elections?election_date=2026-05-19`, verify called with `election_date=date(2026, 5, 19)`
   - `test_election_date_invalid_returns_422` -- GET `/elections?election_date=not-a-date`, expect 422
   - `test_election_date_returns_200` -- GET `/elections?election_date=2026-05-19`, expect 200

6. `TestCombinedFilters` (FILT-04):
   - `test_q_and_county_combined` -- GET `/elections?q=primary&county=Bibb`, verify both params passed
   - `test_race_category_and_date` -- GET `/elections?race_category=federal&election_date=2026-05-19`, verify both passed
   - `test_all_new_filters_combined` -- GET `/elections?q=senate&race_category=state_senate&county=Fulton&election_date=2026-05-19`, verify all 4 passed

7. `TestBackwardCompatibility` (INTG-02):
   - `test_no_new_params_returns_200` -- GET `/elections` with no new params, expect 200 (same as before)
   - `test_existing_district_filter_still_works` -- GET `/elections?district=senate`, verify `district="senate"` passed
   - `test_existing_date_range_still_works` -- GET `/elections?date_from=2026-01-01&date_to=2026-12-31`, verify both passed
   - `test_new_params_default_to_none` -- GET `/elections`, verify `list_elections` called with `q=None, race_category=None, county=None, election_date=None`
